Portsmouth F.C.
Portsmouth F.C. is an English football club.
The club plays in the Football League Championship. Their home stadium is called Fratton Park.
Pompey, also nicknamed the Blues, won the Asian cup in 2008 in a dramatic penalty shootout with Liverpool FC.
Portsmouth's manager is Richie Barker. The most famous manager was probably Harry Redknapp who left the club to go to Tottenham Hotspur. He also won the FA Cup with the club in 2008.
