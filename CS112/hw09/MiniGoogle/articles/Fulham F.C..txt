Fulham F.C.
Fulham Football Club, is an English football club from London. It plays in a white shirt and black shorts for home games. For away games, it plays in blue shirts and white shorts in 2005, but has played in colours such as green, yellow or black. The team's away kit in {(2012)} is black with a gold sash. Mohamed Al-Fayed owns the club. He is from Egypt, and also owns Harrods, a famous shop in London.
