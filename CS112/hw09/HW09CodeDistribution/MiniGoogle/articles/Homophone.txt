Homophone
Homophones are words that sound the same as another word but have a different meaning, and often a different spelling. Examples:
fore, four
it's, its
there, their, they're
Knight, night
which, witch
pore, poor, pour,
pear, pair, pare
see, sea, C
high, hi
wear, where
hare, hair
air, heir
whether, weather
too, to, two
ate, eight
no, know
by, buy, bye
flour, flower
right, write
be, bee
and, end
week, weak
rains, reins
