Iran
Iran (Persian: ايران), once known as Persia, is a country in South-central Asia part of the Middle East region. It shares borders with Afghanistan, Armenia, Azerbaijan, Iraq, Pakistan, Turkey, and Turkmenistan.
Tehran is the capital and its biggest city. Iran is the 18th largest country in the world and has more than 70 million people. Iran is a member of UN (since 1945) and OPEC. It is one of four countries that are Islamic republics, because Islam is the state religion.
