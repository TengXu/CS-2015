Omnivore
Omnivores are animals that eat plants and animals. For example, bears are omnivores, they eat plants, berries, fish and hunt smaller animals. Other examples are:
Mammals
many pigs
many bears
some primates
domestic dogs and cats (although also listed as carnivorous, when in the wild)
some rodents
opossums

Birds
Rails
Rhea
most Seagulls
