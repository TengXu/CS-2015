Bangalore
Bangalore (English Pronunciation:ˈbæŋɡəlɔər, bæŋɡəˈlɔər), also known as Bengaluru (Kannada: ಬೆಂಗಳೂರು) is the capital city of the Indian state of Karnataka. It is famous for its Information technology industry and is called the Silicon Valley of India. Today as a large and growing city, Bangalore has many of the most well-recognized colleges and research institutions in India.
