Java
There is also the Java programming language for computers.

Java (Indonesian: Jawa) is one of the islands of Indonesia. The area is 132,000 square km. It is the world's 13th largest island. Around 140 million people live there. The capital city of Indonesia, Jakarta, is in Java.
The climate of the island is warm and wet. The northern coast of the island is low-lying. The soil is fertile. It is an important area for growing crops in Indonesia.
