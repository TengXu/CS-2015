Power
Power has different meanings when talking about different subjects:
Power (physics), how fast energy can be changed into work. Power is measured in watts.
In sociology, power is when a person tells another to do something, and they do it. This might also be called influence or control.
In politics, power is when one country makes another country do something. This kind of power might come from having a big army, or having a lot of money.
Electric power, elecricity that comes from power plants or generators.
Statistical power, how likely the test is to say something is true when it is really true.
Exponentiation; in mathematics, "power" is multiplying a number by itself. In the expression 23, where we multiply 3 2's together ( 2 * 2 * 2 ) we say that 2 is raised to the power of 3.
A life force, called chi by the Chinese and kiai by the Japanese.
