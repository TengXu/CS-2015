George W. Bush
George Walker Bush (born July 6, 1946) is an American politician and businessman who was the 43rd President of the United States from 2001 to 2009. He is a Republican. He has a ranch in Crawford, Texas, which is near Waco. He is the son of former U.S. President George H. W. Bush and former U.S. First Lady Barbara Bush. Before he was President, Bush was Governor of Texas from 1995 to 2000. He is married to Laura, and they have twin daughters, Jenna and Barbara. He is the brother of Jeb, the former Governor of Florida and Dorothy.
