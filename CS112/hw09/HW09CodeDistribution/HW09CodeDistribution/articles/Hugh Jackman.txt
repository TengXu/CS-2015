Hugh Jackman
Hugh Jackman (born October 12, 1968) is an Australian actor. He was born in Sydney. He is most well-known as the fictional comics character Wolverine in the movie X-Men and its sequels X2, X-Men: The Last Stand. In X-Men Origins: Wolverine, Jackman plays the older Wolverine while Troye Sivan plays the younger one. He is also well known in Australia for his work in musical theatre
