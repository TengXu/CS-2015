Suffix
A suffix is a few letters put at the end of a word to change its meaning.  It is a type of affix.
Verb examples:
-ed
-er
-ing
-s
-ing
walk
walked
walker
walking
walks
Adjective examples:
-er
-est
easy
easier
easiest
Compare to prefix. Prefixes are letters put at the beginning of a word to change its meaning.
