Pan
Pan can mean:
Pan (mythology), a god in Greek mythology
Pan (moon), one of Saturn's moons.
Pan, a genus of apes which has the Common Chimpanzee and the Bonobo
A pan is a metal container usually with one long handle. It is used in cooking. There are several special types: Saucepan, Skillet/Frying pan, Wok, Casserole, Roasting pan, Broiler pan/Grill pan
